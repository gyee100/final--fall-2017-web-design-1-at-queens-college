About Me


Born in Manhattan and grew up in a small town in Long Island.
I wondered what's it like to be someone and do something that I
honestly loved. Still to this day, it surprises me to no end of what
I can do. My main objective for my work is my expression of being
free and happy. The photography and my graphic design uses my
feelings to really thrive myself currently. The time of being in
New York allows me to see how the world can truly be rather than
my fake generalizations Most of my work comes from the day-to-day
practice and my studies as a student.
