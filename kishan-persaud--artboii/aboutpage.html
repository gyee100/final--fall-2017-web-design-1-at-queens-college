about

It’s hard to place when I began to draw, it was all
quite a blur as a child. Surrounded by various
action cartoons and superhero comics it’s no
wonder I dreamed to be a hero. Those dreams
became drawings and, whether the characters I
adored or creations of my own, they always
found their way on paper with the help of my
crayons. Crayons became markers and markers
became fine paints. For a long time, art has been
a defining feature in my life and I believe there’s
no better feeling than expressing myself
through it. It may have started out as a hobby
and it has always been fun to me, but I take
great pride in addressing it at one of my
passions.
